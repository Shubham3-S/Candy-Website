About

About Content

Reese's Chocolate has been delighting taste buds since the 1920s, when it was created by H.B. Reese in Pennsylvania. Mixing creamy peanut butter with smooth milk chocolate, Reese's became a local hit. Today, it's a worldwide favorite, loved for its perfect blend of flavors. From a small kitchen to candy aisles everywhere, Reese's Chocolate is a delicious piece of history you can enjoy anytime!
